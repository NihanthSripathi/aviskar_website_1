ABOUT AVISHKAR :

Avishkar, the flagship event of IEEE - VBIT SB, is a Technical Paper Presentation Competition held every year, exclusively for the first-year students of VBIT. In the year 2011, IEEE - VBIT SB was awarded the eminent "Darrel Chong Gold Student Activity Award" for organizing Avishkar, five years in a row. It exhorts students to ameliorate their presentational acquaintance and acquire expertise in leading-edge technologies. Avishkar indues students with the eloquence of language, enabling them to enhance their oratory skills and research ingenuity.
